-Notes:
Steamed eggs really. steam at pressure, then shock blanch

-Ingredients:
Eggs- any number


-Steps:
1-  Put rack or something on bottom of instant pot
    Arrange Edgs not touching each other if possible

2-  pressure cook on high for
    - 2-4 min for runny eggs
    - 5-7 min for hard boiled eggs

3-  When timer finishes wait 5 minutes
    Vent after 5 minutes

4-  Remove lid
    pull out inner pan and place in sink
    run cold water into the pan
    allow to overflow until water is cold
    wait around 5 minutes

5-  OPTIONAL:
    Peel the eggs underwater by rolling the eggs against the side of the pot, then removing shells

6-  Remove, then eat
